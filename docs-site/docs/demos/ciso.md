# CISO Demo

## Goal

Show CyberArmor.AI as an evidence-driven AI security control layer for leaders
who need visibility, governance, and proof.

## Seed

```bash
bash scripts/demo/run_ciso_demo.sh
```

## Flow

1. Open Customer Portal > Mission Control.
2. Show tenant readiness and next best actions.
3. Open Incidents and explain the AI policy decision record.
4. Open Reports & Evidence Export.
5. Export a summary evidence pack.

## Talk track

CyberArmor.AI helps answer four executive questions:

- What happened?
- Who or what caused it?
- Which policy applied?
- What evidence proves the decision?

## Close

Governance alone is not enough. CyberArmor.AI connects AI governance to
runtime controls and evidence.
