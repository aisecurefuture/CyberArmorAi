## Summary

This proposal covers a fixed-scope TrustOps pilot designed to help `[Client Name]` respond to a customer security questionnaire or due diligence request quickly, cleanly, and with less interruption to internal teams.

The pilot is meant to do two things:

1. Complete one live customer security review efficiently.
2. Create reusable trust-response assets that make future reviews faster.

---

## The Problem

As B2B SaaS companies move into mid-market and enterprise sales, customer security reviews often become a hidden bottleneck. Questionnaires, evidence requests, and follow-up clarifications pull time away from founders, engineers, and sales teams. Answers often live across old spreadsheets, policy documents, and fragmented internal knowledge.

The result is slower deal velocity, inconsistent responses, and avoidable internal overhead.

---

## Pilot Objective

Deliver a customer-ready security questionnaire response package for one live deal while building the first version of a reusable answer library and evidence structure.

---

## Scope

The TrustOps pilot includes:

- completion of `1` customer security questionnaire or due diligence form
- assembly of `1` evidence pack or document index
- creation of `1` reusable answer library seeded from existing materials
- creation of `1` gap summary outlining missing information, unresolved items, or next-step recommendations

This pilot is designed for a single request package and a single review cycle.

---

## Client Responsibilities

To keep the turnaround on track, `[Client Name]` will provide:

- the questionnaire or due diligence request
- any prior questionnaire responses, if available
- existing security and trust materials
- a primary internal point of contact
- one approver for final responses
- prompt responses to clarification questions

---

## Delivery Process

### 1. Intake

We collect the request package, prior answers, and supporting security documents.

### 2. Drafting

We draft source-backed responses, organize evidence, and normalize the answer set.

### 3. Clarification

We provide a concise list of unresolved items or gaps requiring confirmation.

### 4. Finalization

We deliver the completed questionnaire, evidence index, reusable answer base, and gap memo.

---

## Deliverables

At the end of the pilot, `[Client Name]` will receive:

- completed questionnaire draft ready for submission or internal final review
- evidence file list or structured evidence pack
- reusable answer library organized by topic
- concise gap memo with open items and recommended next steps

---

## Timeline

Target turnaround is `3 business days` from the point when all required intake materials are received.

Example:

- **Day 0:** intake confirmed and pilot begins
- **Day 1:** initial drafting and evidence organization
- **Day 2:** clarification questions and review
- **Day 3:** final delivery

Turnaround depends on timely access to materials and internal feedback.
